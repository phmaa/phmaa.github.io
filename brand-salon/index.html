Brand Salon

Welcome to Brand Salon. From the moment you walk in, you will find yourself home with our experienced team of friendly stylists. Right in the heart of downtown Brevard, the "Land of the Waterfalls", Brand Salon offers you an incredible and unique experience to relax and unwind from your daily stresses while getting your hair done.

Brand Salon uses only natural products that have been tested and proven to be the best for your hair. We stay away from anything unnatural or chemical. Come in and ready to be pampered!
